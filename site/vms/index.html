VMS

VCDesign Management Strategy

VMS is an operating protocol for translating management concerns into VCDesign Loops, Boundaries, and Decisions, so responsibility and value continuity can be designed explicitly.

Purpose

VMS exists to clarify where responsibility lives in management decisions and to maximize the continuity of business value.

AI may present alternatives, but final closure always remains with the manager. VMS preserves that premise and closes each consultation as Boundary, Metrics, and Decision.

Monetary

Capital circulation and business continuity. Whether money remains and the business can continue.

Cultural

The history, aesthetics, and cultural value of the domain that must be protected.

Generative

Whether skills, spirit, and decision logic can be handed to the next generation.

Symptom Mapping

The first step is to classify a concern by Loop. This classification is not the answer; it is the entry point for deciding which responsibility boundary to inspect.

Physical Loop

Symptoms: no money left / cash flow is tight

Connects to Unit Economics, Throughput Accounting, and related frames.

Semantic Loop

Symptoms: products do not sell / competitors are winning

Connects to JTBD, Value Proposition Canvas, and related frames.

Value Loop

Symptoms: no passion / unclear reason for continuing

Connects to Ikigai, Purpose-Driven Management, and related frames.

Responsibility Loop

Symptoms: nothing runs without me / successors are not growing

Connects to Generativity, DACI, and related frames.

The first three questions

  • current_pain: What is the hardest thing right now?
  • desire: What would count as success?
  • constraints: Where are the limits of time, money, and mental energy?

Operation

Mode Selection

Maintain

Closure Mode. Protect boundaries and close responsibility.

Expand

Exploration Mode. Rediscover structure and test new hypotheses.

Ambiguous

Diagnostic Exploration. Structure discomfort and bridge it toward Closure.

First Session

  1. Enter the current symptom and identify the closest Loop
  2. Set survival boundaries for cash, mind, and aesthetics
  3. Generate the first Decision for the immediate concern
  4. Select one Metric to monitor at the next review

Minimum Output

Do:
  - Concrete actions to take
Don't:
  - Things to stop immediately
Outcome:
  - The drift expected to improve

boundary_definition:
  - Boundaries created or updated in this decision
metrics_to_watch:
  - Value Drift / Boundary Invasion / Responsibility Deadlock
decision_log:
  owner: final decision owner
  status: Instant / Pending / Discard
  closure_check: which Loop this decision closes

Domain Extensions

VMS is the entry point for management judgment. Domain-specific consultations are handled as knowledge extensions while preserving VMS responsibility principles.

  • VMS Marketing A subset for structuring marketing consultations while preserving options, unknowns, and answer rationale.
