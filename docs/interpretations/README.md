# VCDesign Interpretations

This directory contains explanatory readings of VCDesign for specific audiences.

These documents are not authority specifications.
They do not override files under `specs/`.

## Provenance

These interpretation documents were added on 2026-04-17 as explanatory material
for readers familiar with category theory and related diagrammatic methods.

They were added after the core VCDesign specifications.
They are not part of the original authority structure of VCDesign and do not
change the meaning of files under `specs/`.

They should be read as retrospective interpretation, not as historical origin.

## Purpose

Their purpose is to help readers understand VCDesign through adjacent
frameworks such as category theory, process diagrams, temporal commitment, and
responsibility-preserving composition.

Category theory is used here as an explanatory lens, not as the foundation or
authority of VCDesign.

## Caution

These readings provide structural analogies.

They should not be used to:

- bypass Judgment Closure
- treat AI outputs as accepted decisions
- assume composability where VCDesign requires halt
- replace responsibility with formal structure

VCDesign is not justified by category theory.
It is only explainable through it.
