# Non-Assumptions

UVW deliberately **does not assume** the following:

- **Benevolent Teleology:**  
  The world does not naturally arc toward "better" without effort.
- **Stable Consensus:**  
  "Good" is not universally agreed upon and does not stay agreed upon.
- **AI Understanding:**  
  AI does not "understand" value; it mimics patterns of value descriptions.
- **Permanent Solution:**  
  There is no "final" architecture that solves value alignment once and for all.
